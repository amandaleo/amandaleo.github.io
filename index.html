MALMÖ IDEELLA

This project involved redesigning Malmö Ideella’s logo to make it better suited for their social media channels. The goal was to create a cohesive, platform-friendly design that captures and preserves Malmö Ideella’s core values and visual identity.

This was Malmö Ideella's current logo at the time of the redesign.

Three different logo redesign suggestions were created. To enhance the logo’s visibility on social media, I resized it and adjusted the colors to achieve a more harmonious appearance. Since green is an essential part of Malmö Ideella’s identity, I retained this color and used different shades within the same color scheme to ensure that the colors worked well together.
